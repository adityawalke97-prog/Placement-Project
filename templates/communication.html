Communication Skills Training

Daily English Paragraph

Good communication skills are important for success in every profession. They help us express our ideas clearly and build confidence. To improve communication skills, we should practice speaking English every day, learn new vocabulary, and listen carefully to others.

Interview Communication Tips

  • Introduce yourself confidently.
  • Maintain eye contact while speaking.
  • Speak slowly and clearly.
  • Listen carefully before answering.
  • Use simple and professional language.
  • Smile and maintain positive body language.

Group Discussion Tips

  • Start with a proper introduction.
  • Do not interrupt others.
  • Support your points with examples.
  • Be confident and respectful.
  • Summarize the discussion if possible.

Daily Vocabulary

  • Confident – Feeling sure about yourself.
  • Opportunity – A chance to do something.
  • Communicate – To share information.
  • Professional – Related to a job or career.
  • Achieve – To successfully complete something.

Practice Question

Introduce yourself in English in 5 to 6 sentences and practice it every day.
